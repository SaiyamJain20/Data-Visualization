Marathon Performance Analytics

What is an Ultramarathon?

An ultramarathon is a long-distance running event that exceeds the standard marathon distance of 42.195 km. These races range from 50 km to over 100 km, taking place worldwide in diverse terrains and conditions.

Summary of the Event and Dataset

Ultramarathons attract endurance athletes from around the world, competing in various race formats such as 50 km, 100 km, and beyond. The dataset includes information on event participation, performance metrics, and demographic insights.

The dataset covers details on race distances, number of finishers, participant demographics (age, gender, nationality), and performance trends over time.
